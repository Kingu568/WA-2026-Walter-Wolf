Reactor goes boom

The Chernobyl disaster occurred on April 26, 1986, at the Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant in northern Ukraine, then part of the Soviet Union. A flawed reactor design and operator errors caused a massive explosion and radioactive release. Large areas were contaminated, leading to evacuations and long-term health and environmental consequences. It remains one of the worst nuclear accidents in history.
